Master of Computer Applications (MCA) Degree

About

A Two (02) Year Master Degree Program including Four (04) Semesters. Selection procedure for MCA program is through an Entrance Test.

An MCA degree provides a comprehensive and advanced education in computer science, equipping students with the necessary skills to excel in various roles within the IT industry. Whether pursuing a career in software development, data science, network security, or AI, MCA graduates have a broad spectrum of opportunities to explore and succeed.

Eligibility Criteria

Graduate in any discipline from a recognized University/ Institution with a minimum aggregate of 50% marks and passing Mathematics as one of the subjects in 10+2. Relaxation of 5% marks will be given to the candidates of reserved categories.

Intake Capacity

30 (3 Endowments)

Curriculum

The MCA curriculum generally includes a mix of theoretical and practical subjects aimed at providing a deep understanding of advanced topics in computer science. Common subjects include:

  • Advanced Programming: Java, Python, C#, etc
  • Data Structures and Algorithms
  • DBMS: Design, optimization, administration.
  • Operating Systems: Systems and kernel programming.
  • Software Engineering: Development, project management.
  • Electives: Cloud computing, big data, cybersecurity.
